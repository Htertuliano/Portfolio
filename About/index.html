'the purpose of life is to be defeated by greater and greater things'

-Rene Karl Wilhelm Johann Josef Maria Rilke

Why?

There is little success in defeat, except that it nurtures a wont and will for self-improvement. It creates a space for reflection and allows time for healing. When attempting to build or innovate, you are your worst enemy. When you display your work, praise is not as powerful as pride. One must believe in their work to be fulfilled by it. I believe work should appeal to one's sentiments; it should make them feel, because though many consider sentimentalism trite, emotion is the most powerful of tools...

I tend to shoot for composition. Sharp lines and edges. Architecture is currently my muse.
